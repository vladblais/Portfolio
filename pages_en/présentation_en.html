About myself

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Vladimir and I am currently a first-year computer science student at the IUT of Bordeaux, France. Throughout my studies, I take technical courses in programming, web development, network administration, as well as low-level programming. But I also take courses in project management, economics, law, and communication.

In addition to my computer science program, I am also pursuing two university degrees. The first is an International DU which allows me to take computer science courses in English and offers me the opportunity to do an internship abroad. The second is a DU in Technology for Health Services, in which I learn how to use sensors for healthcare purposes.

In my free time, I also enjoy sports. I have practiced sports throughout my life, but recently, I have turned to climbing. However, I still have an interest in basketball.

After obtaining my Bachelor's degree, I would like to continue my studies and specialize either in mathematical programming or biotechnology informatics, ideally studying abroad.
